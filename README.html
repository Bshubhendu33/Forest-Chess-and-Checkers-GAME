Getting Started

🎮 How to Play

1
Open the game

Click Play Now above, or open index.html directly in any modern browser. No install, no server, no build step needed.

2
Choose your mode

Switch between Chess and Checkers using the toggle in the top bar. Both modes are fully implemented with all standard rules.

3
Click to move

Select a piece to see its legal moves highlighted. Click a highlighted square to move. Captures, castling, and en passant happen automatically.

4
Play online

Hit Create Room to get a 6-character code, share it with your opponent — they open the game, click Join Room, enter the code, and you're connected.
